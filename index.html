Coffee Guide

The best beans to bring home — port by port

Venice · Split · Dubrovnik · Corfu · Naples · Rome · Florence · Nice · Marseille · Barcelona

🇮🇹 Venice

Caffè del Doge

One of the best specialty roasters in Venice. Sells labeled whole bean bags.

Buy
Miscela Espresso / Single Origins
Chocolate Hazelnut Low Acidity

Classic Italian espresso profile. Retail bags available — worth every step.

Calle dei Cinque, San Polo
~20 min walk from Stazione Marittima (1.2 km)

🇭🇷 Split

Croatia has an underrated specialty coffee scene — and this one is steps from the dock.

Macanudo Coffee
Buy
House Specialty Espresso
Smooth Chocolatey Low Acidity

Specialty beans, great staff. Easiest pickup of the whole trip.

Obrov 6, near Riva waterfront
~5 min walk from cruise berth — basically at the dock

🇭🇷 Dubrovnik

One of Croatia's best specialty roasters.

Cogito Coffee
Buy
Cogito House Espresso
Caramel Chocolate Balanced

Very drinkable. Balanced espresso blend.

Old Town
54 min walk from Gruž Port (3.5 km) — taxi recommended (~11 min)

🇬🇷 Corfu

Greek coffee culture is strong. Espresso roasting is newer but solid.

Coffee Island
Buy
Brazil Single Origin
Nutty Smooth Versatile
Multiple locations on the island
33 min walk from Corfu Cruise Terminal (2.3 km)

🇮🇹 Naples

Naples might be the espresso capital of the world.

Caffè Gambrinus
Buy
Gambrinus House Blend (whole bean)
Chocolate Spice Creamy Body

Historic, iconic Neapolitan espresso since 1860. Sells whole bean bags at the counter.

Via Chiaia 1-2, Piazza del Plebiscito
~15 min walk from Molo Beverello (1 km)
⭐ If they only bring you one bag from the whole trip — this is the one.

🇮🇹 Rome

Iconic Roman coffee shop.

Sant'Eustachio Il Caffè
Buy
Regina dei Caffè
Sweet Chocolatey Classic Roman Blend
Near the Pantheon
Day trip — 1h 7min by shuttle/taxi from Civitavecchia port (72 km)

🇮🇹 Florence

Some of Italy's best modern roasters.

Ditta Artigianale
Buy
Jump Espresso Blend
Chocolate Caramel

Modern approach, still great for espresso.

Several locations in Florence
Day trip — 1h 30min by shuttle/taxi from Livorno port (104 km)

🇫🇷 Villefranche-sur-Mer / Nice

The best roaster nearby is in Nice.

Kawa Coffee
Buy
Kawa Espresso Blend
Complex Balanced

One of France's best specialty roasters.

27 min by taxi from Villefranche tender dock — or 51 min by bus/train

🇫🇷 Marseille

Caïpi Coffee
Buy
House Espresso Blend
Chocolate Almond French Roast Character

French specialty roast, sells whole beans. Le Panier neighborhood is worth the walk anyway.

Le Panier neighborhood
~20 min walk from Terminal Croisières (1.5 km)

🇪🇸 Barcelona

One of Europe's best coffee scenes.

Satan's Coffee Corner
Buy
House Single-Origin Espresso
Sweet Balanced Compact retail bags

Exceptional single-origin espresso. Compact bags — perfect for travel.

Arc de Triomf area, Gothic Quarter
~20 min walk from Port Vell (1.5 km)
🏆 Premium option: Nomad Coffee (Poblenou) — ~35 min walk or 11 min taxi. Best roaster in Spain.
